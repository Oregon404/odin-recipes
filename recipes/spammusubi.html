Spam Musubi

Description

This is a simple traditional Hawaiian recipe made from spam, rice, and a sweet and salty sauce (similar to teriyaki sauce) made with soy sauce and sugar.

Ingredients

  • 1 can Spam
  • 2 cups Cooked Short Grain Rice (Sushi Rice)
  • 3 large Nori Sheets
  • 3 tbs Soys sauce
  • 2 tbs Suggar
  • 3 tbs oil
  • 4 Eggs (optional)

Steps

  1. First cook your rice by following instructions on bag
  2. Grab your can of spam and slice it into 9 pieces
  3. In a saucepan mix 3tbs of soy sauce and 2tbs of sugar, heat until sugar is dissolved.
  4. Put your slices of spam in the sauce and simmer for a few minutes per side. Once done set aside
  5. (This part is optional) If you'd like beat 4 eggs
  6. (This part is optional) Add oil to the same saucepan as before and let it heat up
  7. (This part is optional) Once hot add eggs and try to live it as intact as possible
  8. (This part is optional) For extra crunchy Spam Mussubis you can add more oil to the pan and fry up the spam a little bit
  9. Take 3 large sheets of Nori and cut in half
  10. Put some rice into the mold
  11. take your rice block and set on-top of a strip of Nori
  12. if you are using eggs now would be the time to cut them into 9 pisces and set on of those ontop of the rice
  13. Finally add a slice of spam and wrap the Nori around the whole thing. (Tip you can use some rice bits to help keep the Nori shut)
  14. Repeat assembly process un till all your spam slices are gone
