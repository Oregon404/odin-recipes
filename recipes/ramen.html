Ramen

Description

This is a staple college cuisine.

Ingredients

  • 1 Instant Ramen
  • 3 cups Water
  • Veggies (Optional)
  • 2 Medium Boiled Eggs (Optional)
  • Chives To taste (Optional)

Steps

  1. Boil 3 cups of water in a saucepan
  2. add 1 packet of ramen and soup packet to boiling water
  3. Cook ramen for 3 min or as directed on packet
  4. Put in a bowl and add veggies and egg if desired
